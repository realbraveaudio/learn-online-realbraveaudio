Online piano lessons (live 1-on-1)

A guide to starting piano with a live teacher online.

Answer (canonical)

Live online piano lessons work best when lessons focus on reading, rhythm, and a weekly practice plan that matches the student’s age and goals.

What to do next

  1. Get a keyboard/piano with weighted keys if possible.
  2. Schedule weekly lessons.
  3. Use short daily practice sessions.
