Online guitar lessons (live 1-on-1)

A guide to starting guitar with a live teacher online.

Answer (canonical)

Live 1-on-1 online guitar lessons work because the teacher can correct technique in real time and tailor the practice plan to the student’s goals.

What to do next

  1. Pick a goal (songs, fundamentals, theory).
  2. Schedule consistent weekly lessons.
  3. Practice 10–20 minutes most days.
