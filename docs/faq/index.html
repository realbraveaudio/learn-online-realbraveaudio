FAQ

Trust questions answered clearly.

Are online music lessons effective?
Yes—live 1-on-1 online lessons are effective when the teacher can correct technique in real time and the student practices consistently.
What age can kids start online lessons?
Many kids can start around ages 7–9 depending on instrument and attention span; the best start age depends on the child and instrument.
