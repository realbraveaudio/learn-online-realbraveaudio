FAQ (AEO)

Quick answers

Clear, direct answers to the questions people actually ask.

Do you teach kids and adults in Queens?

Yes. We teach kids (6+), teens, and adults, from beginner to advanced, including homeschool families and special needs students.

What is PracticePad?

PracticePad is our live 1:1 lesson portal plus a video recording and progress tracking interface. Students can rewatch lessons, follow clear assignments, and parents can see exactly what to practice.

Is this just for beginners?

No. Beginners love the fast wins, and experienced students use the system to tighten technique and build performance confidence faster.

Where do students perform?

Students participate in showcases, open mics, and band programs. The goal is stage-ready confidence, not just lesson attendance.
